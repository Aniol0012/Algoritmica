Messi's brother asks for forgiveness following his shots against Barcelona, its fans and the Catalans
Ansu Fati's Barcelona future is in his own hands
Lionel Messi has yet to extend his contract with Paris Saint-Germain and Barcelona fans still wish that their former captain could return to Catalonia.
Those who heard what his brother, Matias Messi, said on Twitch may not be so confident though, as he stated that the Argentina star won't go back to the Blaugrana while Joan Laporta is the president.
